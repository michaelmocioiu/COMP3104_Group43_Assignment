about the importance of learning DevOps in the software development cycle

Learning devops will help us understand a couple of different things 
for which we could implement to and add on to our skills to better prepare 
us for the real-world environment and its competetive nature

- we will be able better respond to market demands to stay competetive by
  integrating DevOps practices
- we will be able to better communicate and efficiently work with development
  and operative teams by integrating DevOps practices, allowing us to quickly 
  identify and address concerns and issues
- we will learn to integrate automated testing and monitoring into our project's
  development processes which leads to more reliable software with fewer bugs and
  issues
  
It is a definite must for all software engineers to understand and implement DevOps
practices and principles to improve their software perfomance and overall value as a
team member
