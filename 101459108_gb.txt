George Brown College Information

George Brown College (GBC) is a reputable post-secondary institution located in Toronto, Ontario.
It has several campuses: 
  - Casa Loma
  - St. James
  - Waterfront
And they are all beautiful!

GBC offers many different education programs from manufacturing to culinary studies.
The school is well known for having great industry connections, granting students head starts to their careers during their education!
