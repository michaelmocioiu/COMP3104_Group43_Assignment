COMP3104 - DevOps Information
This course is a third year, first semester course at George Brown College.

Its key objectives are:
  - To teach students about various concepts and methods in the software development cycle
  - Train students in using GitHub
  - Train students in using CI tools like TravisCI or GitHub Actions
  - Train students in deployment strategies
  - Analyze the marker as a DevOps engineer
  - Teach students about version control

Delivery Method: 
Labs and Lectures are online, accessible through D2L and hosted on Zoom. 
Assignments are posted and submitted online through D2L
